Confirmation of Funds (POF)

This financial instrument confirms that clients or the company have the capabilities and declared assets, funds at their disposal, available for use in the transaction. The document is delivered in the form of a bank statement, a bank statement or an extract from the asset register. The purpose of the document is to confirm that the funds necessary for the transaction are available and obtained legally. It is used to conclude agreements with banks and large companies.

REL IG provides “customer-prepared” funds confirmation solutions (POFs) to its customers who need to take steps to confirm their funds or financial ability to complete a transaction.
