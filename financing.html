Our financing

Obtaining credit funds from international banks, the process is very long and complicated. To attract investment financing for projects, our company uses the mechanism of organizing a "joint venture" with the customer’s company. Under such a “joint venture”, POLINVES 20 LTD, in a short time and on fairly flexible terms, can arrange loan (investment) financing for a customer’s project, providing financial instruments, such as: Bank Guarantee (BG) , Standby Letter of Credit (SBLC) , Certificate of Deposit (CD) and others.

Our company can offer the most competitive conditions with the assistance in organizing the financing of various business projects. The decision on project financing is made individually for each project and is absolutely confidential.

Borrowed financing against the provision of financial instruments provides customers with a wide opportunity to obtain financing for their projects, this simplifies the requirements for the current financial condition of the client and the quality of the financed project than traditional financing.
