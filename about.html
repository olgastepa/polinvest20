Our services

«POLINVES 20» LTD provides consulting services to companies wishing to attract various financial instruments for the development of their business or to receive financing for their projects. We offer companies that, due to insufficient volume of their own assets, cannot receive the necessary bank financing for their projects. For this, we offer a full range of services for attracting an investment loan, securing financial instruments or financing for transferring projects to corporate governance. Often, our customers do not need full coverage of the cost of the issued instruments, but only compensation of the cost of the costs of its production is needed.

Our company, having close, tested relationships and the support of various management companies, international foundations and financial institutions, offers an individual approach for each client and establishing partnerships. We are ready to quickly and efficiently respond to customer requests using our innovative financial strategies.
